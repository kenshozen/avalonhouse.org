Programming

Avalon is holding a soirée to welcome in the summer. Expect live music, cocktails, conversation, and discussion of Avalon's upcoming plans. Please bring flowers in some form: garlands, crowns, pinned blooms, embroidered or printed flowers, or simply in a bouquet.

At 8:20 PM as the sun begins to set there will be a reading of poems celebrating the season, bring fitting tributes from your favorite poets. There will be periodic performances of music throughout the evening. Plus ones are welcome; please consult the hosts before forwarding the invite further. We look forward to seeing you there!
