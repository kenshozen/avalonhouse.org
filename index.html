The Avalon Institute

Avalon is dedicated to creating the intimacy necessary for the collective pursuit of truth.

◇

Avalon continuously experiments with formats that cultivate and communicate passion, building the network and infrastructure needed to found a new home for the revival of art and thought.

As institutional decay and atomization degrade the infrastructure meant to enable the next generation of thought and art, new contexts must be created where they can find the fellowships they need to build a future.  Avalon's purpose is to provide them.

Avalon House is Avalon's current San Francisco base: a home in Twin Peaks for the open-ended pursuit of truth and the production of events and cultural life. Since September 2024, it has hosted poetry readings, string quartets, acted Platonic dialogues, lectures, weekend seminars, symposia, and brought together scholars from around the world.

Avalon Institute builds the capacity needed to draw enduring work into the world. Through technical and media support, publications, and the cultivation of a network, Avalon turns fellowship into a foundation. We work to maintain an ecology of collaboration and exchange where many projects can carve out a home.

We co-host with organisations that share a deep intellectual or cultural focus. Reach out →
